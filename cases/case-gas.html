1. Gioi thieu cong ty

GAS (PV GAS) la cong ty khi lon nhat Viet Nam, gio vi tri doc quyen thuc te trong viec thu mua va phan phoi khi tu nhien. GAS la "duong ong huyet mach" cua nganh nang luong VN - neu GAS ngung hoat dong, nhieu nha may dien va phan bon se bị te liet.

Co so ha tang

He thong duong ong khi:
  Nam Con Son 1 & 2: ~400km, cong suat 7 ty m3/nam
  PM3 - Ca Mau:      ~325km, cong suat 2 ty m3/nam
  Bach Ho:           ~100km, mo cu Bach Ho

Cac cum nha may khi:
  Cum nha may Phu My (Binh Duong):
    - Phu My 1, 2.1, 2.2, 3, 4 (Dien, phan bon, hoa chat)
    - Tong cong suat khi ~6-7 ty m3/nam

  Cum nha may Ca Mau (Ca Mau):
    - DPM, DCM nhan khi tu GAS
    - Cac nha may dien Ca Mau 1, 2

Kho LNG nhap khau (dang phat trien):
  Thi Vai LNG Terminal: 1 trieu tan/nam LNG (2023 chinh thuc)

Vi the doc quyen

GAS = Infrastructure Monopoly

GAS so huu toan bo he thong duong ong khi quoc gia. Khong ai co the van chuyen khi o VN ma khong qua GAS. Day la "moat" khong the pha vo - chi phi xay dung lai duong ong la vo cung lon, khong co nha dau tu tu nhan nao dam lam. Dieu nay giup GAS co bieu do bien loi nhuan on dinh hon nhieu so voi PVD hay BSR.

  - Nha may phan bon (DPM, DCM) → ~20% san luong
  - Cong nghiep (lo hoi, hoa chat) → ~15% san luong
  - LPG, CNG, LNG ban le → ~15% san luong

Co che gia khi cua GAS

GAS mua khi tu cac mo voi gia cong thuc (thuong lien ket % gia dau Brent) va ban cho khach hang voi gia khac. Bien loi nhuan GAS phu thuoc vao CHECH LECH GIA BAN - GIA MUA. Khi gia dau tang manh dot ngot, GAS co the bi "ep" giua viec mua gia cao nhung ban gia cu (hop dong cu), tao ra rui ro bien mong ngan han.

GAS = Co phieu co tuc hang dau

GAS co lich su tra co tuc tien mat deu dan hang nam, thuong tu 2,500-5,000 dong/co phieu. Do von hoa lon va dong tien on dinh, GAS la lua chon hang dau cho nha dau tu dai han muon thu nhap thu dong tu co tuc. Day la diem khac biet lon so voi PVD (co tuc khong on dinh) va BSR (chua co tuc).

4. Metrics dac thu: San luong khi, Gia khi, Hop dong

4.1 San luong khi ban (ty m3/nam)

San luong khi ban theo nam (vi du tham khao):
  2019: ~9.5 ty m3
  2020: ~8.7 ty m3 (Covid, nha may dien giam tieu thu)
  2021: ~9.0 ty m3
  2022: ~8.8 ty m3 (khi Ru May va cac mo nho giam)
  2023: ~8.5 ty m3 (mo cu giam, chua co nguon bu)

Xu huong: Mo khi cu (Nam Con Son, Bach Ho) dang suy giam
→ PV GAS can:
  (1) Tim mo khi moi trong nuoc (Block B - O Mon)
  (2) Tang nhap khau LNG de bu
→ RUNG LON: Neu Block B cham tien do, GAS gap thach thuc san luong

4.2 Gia khi binh quan (USD/MMBtu)

Phan khucCach tinh giaTac dong khi gia dau tang
Khi cho nha may dien Cong thuc lien ket dau Brent Gia ban tang → Doanh thu tang

LPG ban le Saudi Contract Price (CP) Theo sat gia dau quoc te

4.3 Hop dong dai han - Bao dam doanh thu

  • Nha may dien PV Power, EVN: Hop dong Take-or-Pay 15-20 nam - khach hang PHAI mua san luong toi thieu du co dung hay khong
  • DPM, DCM: Hop dong khi dai han voi gia dieu tiet → on dinh nhung bien mong thap
  • LNG nhap khau: Hop dong mua LNG dai han (Qatargas, Venture Global) dang duoc dam phan

4.4 Du an Block B - O Mon: Catalyst lon nhat

Block B - O Mon: Qua bom tang truong cua GAS

Du an Block B - O Mon la du an khi - dien lon nhat Viet Nam, du kien dua khi tu bien vao cac nha may dien O Mon (Can Tho) tu 2025-2026. Neu du an trien khai dung tien do, GAS se co them ~2-3 ty m3 san luong khi moi/nam, tang van toc tang truong loi nhuan dang ke. Theo doi tien do du an nay la viec lam quan trong nhat khi phan tich GAS dai han.

5. Phan tich chu ky

GAS = It chu ky hon PVD

Tai sao GAS bien dong it hon PVD?

GAS co hop dong dai han Take-or-Pay voi cac nha may dien va phan bon - khach hang buoc phai mua san luong toi thieu. Dieu nay tao ra "san doanh thu" on dinh. Tuy nhien, loi nhuan van bien dong theo gia dau/khi vi cong thuc gia ban lien ket voi gia dau Brent. GAS la co phieu ban chu ky nhung it bien dong hon PVD hay BSR.

Tuong quan voi gia dau

Gia dau Brent tang:
  → Gia khi dau vao tang (GAS mua dat hon)
  → Gia khi ban ra tang (theo cong thuc lien ket)
  → NET: Bien loi nhuan on dinh neu cong thuc thong nhat
  → Doanh thu nominal tang (vi gia ban tang)

Gia dau Brent giam:
  → Doanh thu giam (vi gia ban giam)
  → Nhung chi phi mua khi cung giam
  → NET: Bien loi nhuan kha on dinh

→ GAS hien THUAN LOI khi gia dau ON DINH o muc cao,
  khong phai khi gia dau BIEN DONG manh

Rui ro san luong dai han

  • Mo khi cu (Nam Con Son) suy giam ~5-8%/nam
  • Mo moi (Block B) va LNG can trien khai dung tien do
  • Neu san luong giam ma khong co bu, doanh thu giam boi ca san luong lan gia

7. Dinh gia

Phuong phap dinh gia phu hop

GAS la co phieu co dong tien on dinh va co tuc thuong xuyen, nen co the dung nhieu phuong phap hon PVD hay BSR.

Vi du phan tich Dividend Yield

Gia su co tuc GAS: 3,500 dong/co phieu/nam (deu qua cac nam)
Gia co phieu hien tai: 80,000 dong

Dividend Yield = 3,500 / 80,000 = 4.4%
→ Thap hon lai suat tiet kiem (5-6%) → Co phieu chua hap dan ve co tuc

Neu gia giam ve 55,000 dong:
Dividend Yield = 3,500 / 55,000 = 6.4%
→ Hap dan hon, tuong duong lai tiet kiem + upside gia

→ Voi co phieu co tuc, hay tinh Dividend Yield truoc
   de xac dinh vung gia hop ly

8. Ket luan & Khuyen nghi

Diem manh

  • Vi the doc quyen infrastructure - moat gần như vĩnh cuu
  • Dong tien on dinh tu hop dong Take-or-Pay dai han
  • Co tuc deu dang, co lich su tra thuong xuyen
  • Bang can doi sach, no thap, FCF tot
  • Huong loi tu tang truong nhu cau khi (dien, phan bon, cong nghiep)

Diem yeu / Rui ro

  • San luong khi tu mo noi dia dang suy giam tu nhien
  • Phu thuoc tien do Block B va LNG - neu cham tre thi bi anh huong
  • Rui ro chuyen dich nang luong xanh (dai han)
  • Free float thap (<5%) - kho mua so luong lon, thanh khoan han che
  • Chinh sach gia khi Chinh phu co the can thiep bat loi

Khung khuyen nghi

GAS - Co phieu giu dai han

Phan loai: GAS la co phieu GIU DAI HAN phu hop cho nha dau tu muon on dinh, co tuc. Khong phai co phieu "trading" ngan han. Mua khi Dividend Yield >6%, P/E <12x, va tien do Block B tich cuc. Giu khi gia hop ly, theo doi tien do Block B va muc co tuc hang nam. Xem xet giam neu co bang chung Block B bi hoan vo han dinh hoac chinh sach khi bat loi nhieu lan.
