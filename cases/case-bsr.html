1. Gioi thieu cong ty

BSR van hanh Nha may Loc dau Dung Quat (Quang Ngai) - nha may loc dau duy nhat cua Viet Nam tinh den 2024. Day la cong trinh co so ha tang chien luoc quoc gia, duoc xay dung voi muc tieu giam phu thuoc nhap khau xang dau thanh pham.

Nha may Loc dau Dung Quat

Vi tri: Khu kinh te Dung Quat, Quang Ngai
Cong suat: 6.5 trieu tan dau tho/nam (130,000 thung/ngay)
Von dau tu ban dau: ~3 ty USD (hoan thanh 2009)

San pham loc dau:
  Xang RON 92, RON 95:     ~30% cong suat
  Diesel 0.05% S:           ~30% cong suat
  Kerosene / Jet fuel:      ~7% cong suat
  LPG (khi hoa long):       ~5% cong suat
  Polypropylene (nhua PP):  ~3% cong suat
  Condensate, dau FO:       ~25% cong suat

Chuyen doi 2024-2025: Nang cap de xu ly dau tho nhap khau
  (Dung Quat v1 chi xu ly duoc dau nhe, ngot - chiet khau thap)
  (Sau nang cap: xu ly duoc dau nang, chua - linh hoat hon)

Vi the dac biet: Nha may loc dau duy nhat

BSR - Nha san xuat xang dau noi dia duy nhat

BSR cung cap 30-35% nhu cau xang dau noi dia cua Viet Nam. Phan con lai la nhap khau. Day la vi the chien luoc nhung khong phai "moat" ve loi nhuan - BSR van phai canh tranh gia voi xang nhap khau, duoc dieu chinh boi Bo Cong Thuong. Nha may loc dau thu 2 (Nghi Son - NSR) cua Thanh Hoa (lien doanh voi Kuwait, Idemitsu) da van hanh tu 2018, la canh tranh trong nuoc.

  - Diesel (30%): Cong nghiep, van tai, dien

Khach hang

  • PV Oil (OIL): Khach hang lon thu hai, he thong phan phoi rong
  • Cac hang hang khong (VNA, VJA, BBA): Mua jet fuel
  • Cac nha may dien: Mua dau FO cho lo dot
  • Cong ty nhua: Mua Polypropylene
BSR co the tu dinh gia ban khong?

Khong! Gia ban xang dau cua BSR phai canh tranh voi gia nhap khau (do Bo Cong Thuong dieu chinh). PLX va OIL se mua tu nguon re hon giua BSR va nhap khau. BSR thuong duoc huong loi khi gia xang noi dia duoc nha nuoc dieu chinh tre so voi gia dau tho (gian tri thap hon thi truong). Day la diem phuc tap nhat trong mo hinh BSR.

Dac diem bien loi nhuan BSR

Bien loi nhuan BSR - Mong nhat trong nganh dau khi

BSR co bien EBITDA truoc khi tinh lai/lo ton kho chi khoang 4-8% - rat mong so voi PVD (15-35%) hay GAS (15-20%). Dieu nay co nghia la mot bien dong nho cua crack spread hay gia dau tho co the quet sach loi nhuan. Nam 2020 la dien hinh: gia dau sup do dot ngot → BSR lo khung 3,500 ty dong. Day la dac trung cua nganh loc dau toan cau - bien mong, don bay cao.

Co tuc

BSR hau nhu khong tra co tuc. Cong ty dung toan bo loi nhuan de tra no vay xay dung nha may va nang cap. Day la ly do BSR khong phu hop cho nha dau tu muon co tuc. Mua BSR la mua ky vong gia tang gia tri thi truong theo chu ky crack spread.

4. Metrics dac thu: Crack Spread, GRM, Utilization, Lai/Lo ton kho

4 chi so bat buoc khi phan tich BSR

Neu ban chi nam duoc 4 chi so nay, ban da co the phan tich duoc 80% bien dong loi nhuan BSR: (1) Crack Spread, (2) GRM, (3) Utilization rate, (4) Lai/Lo ton kho.

4.1 Crack Spread - Linh hon cua nganh loc dau

CRACK SPREAD = Gia san pham xang dau - Gia dau tho dau vao

Vi du don gian (3-2-1 crack spread):
  3 thung dau tho → 2 thung xang + 1 thung diesel
  Gia dau tho:  3 x $80 = $240
  Gia xang ban: 2 x $100 = $200
  Gia diesel:   1 x $110 = $110
  3-2-1 crack spread = (200+110-240)/3 = $23.3/thung

Bien dong crack spread theo lich su:
  - Thap (thi truong loc du):    $5-10/thung → BSR loi nhuan sat 0
  - Trung binh:                  $10-18/thung → BSR co loi nhuan hop ly
  - Cao (2022):                  $20-35/thung → BSR loi nhuan dot bien
  - Rat cao (khung hoang cung):  >$35/thung (hiem)

Nguyen nhan crack spread tang:
  + Thieu cong suat loc dau toan cau (nha may dong, it dau tu moi)
  + Nhu cau xang dau phuc hoi manh sau Covid
  + Nga bi trung phat → xang diesel chau Au thieu hut

Nguyen nhan crack spread giam:
  - Kinh te suy cham → nhu cau xang giam
  - Nha may loc dau moi di vao hoat dong
  - Mua dong xuan (nhu cau heating oil thap)

4.2 GRM - Bien loc thuc te cua BSR

GRM (Gross Refining Margin) = Doanh thu ban san pham
                              - Chi phi dau tho mua vao
                              (Tinh tren moi thung dau xu ly)

GRM cua BSR 2022: ~$15-22/thung (rat cao)
GRM cua BSR 2023: ~$4-6/thung (binh thuong)
GRM trung binh lich su BSR: ~$5-8/thung

→ De tinh nhanh loi nhuan:
  EBITDA = GRM x San luong xu ly (tan)
  GRM = $6/thung ≈ $44/tan (1 tan ≈ 7.33 thung)
  San luong: 6.5 trieu tan/nam
  → EBITDA ~ $286 trieu ≈ 6,900 ty VND (ti gia 24,000)

4.3 Utilization Rate (Ty le su dung nha may)

  • >95%: Hieu qua toi da - chi phi co dinh duoc phan bo tot
  • 85-95%: Binh thuong - co thoi gian bao tri va dung may theo ke hoach
  • <80%: Kho khan - chi phi co dinh tang, hieu suat giam
  • BSR thuong van hanh o 90-100% (khi khong co bao tri lon)
  • Lich bao tri lon (Turnaround): ~2-3 thang moi 4-5 nam → giam doanh thu dang ke

4.4 Lai/Lo ton kho - Bien dong khon luong

Lai/Lo ton kho - Thu "phu" nhung to bang "chinh"

BSR thuong truyen giu khoang 15-25 ngay ton kho dau tho (can thiet de van hanh lien tuc). Khi gia dau TANG, ton kho nay tang gia → Lai ton kho (loi nhuan ao, khong co tien mat). Khi gia dau GIAM dot ngot, ton kho giam gia → Lo ton kho (lo that su, phai trich lai tren BCTC). Nam 2020: BSR lo ~1,500-2,000 ty chi rieng phan lo ton kho vi gia dau giam cuc nhanh. Day la khanh niem khong the bo qua khi doc BCTC BSR.

Vi du tinh Lai/Lo ton kho:
  Ton kho dau tho: 500,000 tan (~$40 trieu)
  Gia dau khi mua vao: $80/thung
  Gia dau hien tai: $65/thung (giam $15)

  Lo ton kho (concept):
  = 500,000 tan x 7.33 thung/tan x ($65-$80)
  = 3,665,000 thung x (-$15)
  = -$55 trieu ≈ -1,320 ty VND

  → Dai voi LNST BSR co the chi la 2,000-3,000 ty/nam
    → 1 dot giam gia dau co the xoa sach 30-50% loi nhuan

5. Phan tich chu ky

Crack spread - Chu ky rieng, khong giong gia dau

HIEU NHAM PHAN TICH BSR THEO GIA DAU:
  Nhieu NĐT nghi: Gia dau tang → BSR loi (SAI!)
  Thuc te: Gia dau tang → chi phi dau vao tang → BSR co the bi squeez

  DUNG PHAN TICH BSR THEO CRACK SPREAD:
  Crack spread tang (san pham dat hon dau tho) → BSR loi
  Crack spread giam → BSR kho

  Gia dau tang NHANH va MANH: BSR bi lo ton kho ngan han
  Gia dau giam NHANH va MANH: BSR lo ton kho ngan han
  Gia dau ON DINH o bat ky muc nao: BSR huong loi tu crack spread

→ BSR LAI NHAT khi gia dau ON DINH va CRACK SPREAD CAO

7. Dinh gia

Kho dinh gia nhat trong nhom dau khi

BSR - Co phieu kho dinh gia nhat

BSR la co phieu kho dinh gia nhat vi: (1) Bien loi nhuan dao dong tu am den 5% - EPS bien dong lon, (2) Lai/lo ton kho tao ra nhieu "nhieu" trong BCTC, (3) Thanh khoan UPCOM thap, (4) PVN so huu 92% - free float rat thap. Nen dung EV/EBITDA (truoc lai/lo ton kho) la phuong phap trung tam.

Phuong phapAp dungMuc he so
EV/EBITDA (truoc ton kho)Tot nhat cho BSR5-8x (khi trung binh), 3-5x (khi thap)
Normalized P/EDung EPS khong gom lai/lo ton kho6-10x EPS chuan hoa
P/BAn toan nhat - book value on dinh hon EPS0.8-1.5x
P/E hien taiTranh dung - EPS dao dong manhKhong nen ap dung

Vi du tinh EV/EBITDA

Gia dinh BSR 2024:
  San luong xu ly: 6.2 trieu tan
  GRM ky vong: $7/thung = $51.3/tan
  EBITDA = 6,200,000 x $51.3 x 24,000 VND/USD
         = ~7,630 ty VND

  EV ky vong (7x EBITDA) = 7,630 x 7 = ~53,400 ty
  No rong (Net Debt) = ~5,000 ty
  Von hoa = EV - Net Debt = ~48,400 ty
  So co phieu: ~3,100 trieu co phieu
  → Gia muc tieu = 48,400,000 / 3,100 = ~15,600 dong/co phieu

Luu y:
  - Neu GRM giam ve $5 → EV/EBITDA target dat hon
  - Neu co lai ton kho lon → EPS thuc te cao hon
  - Nen lam sensitivity analysis: best/base/bear case GRM

8. Ket luan & Khuyen nghi

Diem manh

  • Nha may loc dau duy nhat cua VN tinh den 2024 - co vi the chien luoc
  • Co san phẩm da dang (xang, diesel, LPG, PP, jet fuel)
  • Duoc Nha nuoc ho tro (bao ho khoi hang nhap khau qua chinh sach gia)
  • Du an nang cap Dung Quat tang linh hoat va cong suat
  • Von hoa tuong doi lon, don bay vao chu ky crack spread tot

Diem yeu / Rui ro

  • Bien loi nhuan rat mong - don bay am khi crack spread giam
  • Rui ro lo ton kho co the xoa sach loi nhuan ca nam trong vai tuan
  • Chi xu ly duoc dau nhe ngot (Dung Quat v1) - han che linh hoat nguyen lieu
  • Canh tranh tu Nghi Son (NSR) va xang nhap khau
  • Thanh khoan UPCOM thap - kho giao dich so luong lon
  • Chua co lo trinh niem yet HOSE ro rang - han che co so nha dau tu

Khung khuyen nghi

Khi nao mua BSR?

Dieu kien MUA: (1) Crack spread dang o day va co tin hieu phuc hoi (theo Singapore GRM), (2) Gia dau dang on dinh sau giai doan bien dong, (3) P/B <1x hoac EV/EBITDA <5x, (4) Cac nha may loc dau toan cau dang dong bao tri lon → giam cung cap, (5) Mua du lich/hang khong phuc hoi manh → nhu cau jet fuel tang → crack spread tang.

Tranh BSR khi nao?

TRANH MUA: (1) Gia dau dang tang rat nhanh trong ngan han (rui ro lo ton kho), (2) Crack spread dang o dinh lịch su (>$20/thung) - co the giam nhanh, (3) Nhieu nha may loc dau lon moi tren toan cau di vao van hanh, (4) Kinh te the gioi suy cham → nhu cau xang dau giam, (5) Cac quy dau tu nuoc ngoai ban ra (co tin hieu tu thanh khoan bat thuong tren UPCOM).
