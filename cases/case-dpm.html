1. Gioi thieu cong ty

DPM (Dam Phu My) la nha san xuat ure lon nhat Viet Nam, voi nha may dat tai Phu My, Ba Ria - Vung Tau. Cong ty san xuat ure tu khi tu nhien cung cap boi GAS, tao ra san pham phan bon thiet yeu cho nong nghiep Viet Nam va xuat khau.

Nha may va cong suat

Nha may Dam Phu My (Ba Ria - Vung Tau):
  Cong suat: 800,000 tan ure/nam (cong suat thiet ke)
  San pham: Ure hat trong, ure hat duc, NH3, CO2
  Nguon khi: Tu GAS qua duong ong Nam Con Son

Nha may DPM Phu My NPK (mo rong):
  San pham: Phan bon NPK tong hop
  Cong suat: 250,000 tan NPK/nam
  Muc tieu: Da dang hoa san pham, tang gia tri gia tang

Vi the thi truong:
  - Thi phan ure trong nuoc: ~35-40% (chia se voi DCM ~30%)
  - Nam phan phoi: ~4,500 dai ly toan quoc
  - Xuat khau: 20-30% san luong sang Campuchia, Myanmar, Philippines

Dac thu: Cong ty "hai chieu chu ky"

DPM phu thuoc 2 gia: Gia khi dau vao + Gia ure dau ra

DPM bi anh huong boi 2 chu ky doc lap: (1) Chu ky dau khi - quyet dinh gia khi dau vao (chi phi san xuat), va (2) Chu ky nong san/phan bon - quyet dinh gia ure dau ra (doanh thu). Hai chu ky nay khong dong bo, tao ra do phuc tap trong phan tich. DPM co the hoi phuc khi: gia khi on dinh + gia ure cao.

2. Mo hinh kinh doanh

Quy trinh san xuat

KHI TU NHIEN (tu GAS)
    → Quy trinh Reforming: CH4 + H2O → CO2 + H2
    → Quy trinh Haber-Bosch: N2 + H2 → NH3 (Amoniac)
    → Quy trinh Ure: NH3 + CO2 → (NH2)2CO (URE)
    → Tao hat, dong goi, phan phoi

Chi phi san xuat ure:
  - Khi tu nhien: ~50-60% tong chi phi
  - Nhan cong + van hanh: ~20-25%
  - Khau hao (nha may): ~15-20%

→ Gia khi dau vao la BIEN SO QUAN TRONG NHAT tac dong den BIEN LN

Khach hang va kenh phan phoi

KenhMo ta% Doanh thu
Phan phoi trong nuoc ~4,500 dai ly, nong dan vung DBSCL, Tay Nguyen, DHMT ~70-80%
Xuat khau Campuchia, Myanmar, Philippines, thi truong ASEAN ~20-30%
Cong nghiep Amoniac cho cong nghiep hoa chat, lanh ~3-5%

MUA VU: Nhan to khong the bo qua

Mua vu nong nghiep quyet dinh doanh thu

Nong dan mua ure theo mua vu gieo trong. Tai Viet Nam, 2 mua vu chinh: Dong Xuan (Oct-Feb) va He Thu (Apr-Jul). DPM thong thuong co doanh thu tot Q1 va Q2. Q3 va Q4 thuong thap hon. Khi phan tich BCTC theo quy, can tinh den yeu to mua vu de tranh ke luan sai.

Phan tich bien loi nhuan giai doan 2021-2023

Giai doan sieu loi nhuan 2021-2022:
  - Gia ure thi truong: $400 → $900/tan (+125%)
  - Gia khi dau vao: Van o muc cu (hop dong lau dai voi GAS)
  - → Bien loi nhuan tang nhanh: 9% → 25%

Giai doan suy giam 2023:
  - Gia ure giam: $900 → $300/tan (-67%)
  - Gia khi: Tang do GAS dieu chinh gia
  - → Bien loi nhuan sap: 25% → 11%

BAI HOC: DPM bien dong rat lon theo chu ky gia ure/khi
→ Khong bao gio dung EPS nam ky luc de dinh gia!
→ Phai dung EPS trung binh chu ky (Normalized EPS)
Co tuc DPM - On dinh hay khong?

DPM thuong tra co tuc tien mat hang nam, khoang 1,500-5,000 dong/co phieu tuy theo ket qua kinh doanh. Nam 2022 (ky luc loi nhuan), co tuc len toi 5,000-7,000 dong. Nam 2023 giam ve 1,500-2,000 dong. Co tuc khong on dinh bang GAS nhung thuong xuyen hon PVD.

4. Metrics dac thu: Gia ure, Gia khi, San luong, Mua vu

4.1 Gia ure the gioi (USD/tan)

Thi truong ure quoc te:
  Gia chuan: FOB Yuzhny (Ukraina), FOB Middle East, CFR Vietnam
  Gia dao dong: $200-900/tan (phu thuoc cung-cau toan cau)

Cac nhan to day gia ure tang:
  + Nga/Ukraina xung dot → nguon cung giam
  + Trung Quoc han che xuat khau ure
  + Gia khi chau Au tang → chi phi san xuat EU tang
  + Mua gieo trong toan cau dong thoi (Q1-Q2)
  + Kho du tru thap

Cac nhan to keo gia ure xuong:
  - Trung Quoc mo lai xuat khau
  - Nguon cung tu Trung Dong tang (Iran, Saudi)
  - Nhu cau nong nghiep giam (han han, lu lut)
  - Ton kho lon o cac trung tam phan phoi

Muc gia can theo doi cho DPM:
  > $350/tan: Co loi nhuan tot
  $280-350/tan: Loi nhuan mong, vua du boi chi phi
  < $280/tan: Kho khan, co the lo

Co che bien loi nhuan DPM

Bien LN DPM = Gia ure ban ra - Chi phi khi - Chi phi khac. Day la so can theo doi sat. Khi gia ure tang nhanh hon gia khi, bien no ra. Khi gia ure giam hoac gia khi tang, bien bi squeez. Nam 2023 la vi du dien hinh: gia ure giam 67% trong khi gia khi tang 30% → bien loi nhuan sap manh.

4.3 San luong tieu thu (1000 tan/nam)

  • Cong suat thiet ke: 800,000 tan/nam
  • San luong thuc te thuong dat 90-95% cong suat: ~720,000 - 760,000 tan
  • Khi gia ure thap, co the cat giam san xuat neu gia ban duoi chi phi bien
  • Ton kho cuoi ky la chi bao quan trong - ton kho cao bao hieu ap luc gia

5. Phan tich chu ky

Chu ky gia ure - Phu thuoc 3 nhan to chinh

CUNG (Supply):
  + San xuat ure toan cau: Nga, Trung Quoc, Trung Dong, EU, VN
  + Chinh sach xuat khau cua Nga va Trung Quoc (uu tien noi dia)
  + Gia khi → chi phi san xuat → quyet dinh co dang san xuat khong

CAU (Demand):
  + Dien tich gieo trong toan cau (lien ket gia luong thuc)
  + Mua vu nong nghiep (tap trung Q1-Q2 va Q4)
  + Ton kho o dai ly, trung tam phan phoi

CHOT CU:
  + Gia khi tu nhien quoc te (Henry Hub, TTF chau Au)
  + Chi phi van chuyen (cuoc tau)

→ 2020-2021: Cung giam (EU cat san xuat do gia khi cao) + Cau tang
→ 2021-2022: Gia ure len dinh ~$900/tan
→ 2023: Trung Quoc, Nga tang xuat khau → gia giam ve $280-320/tan

DPM hien o dau trong chu ky?

Nhan dinh vi tri chu ky (2024)

Sau giai doan ky luc 2021-2022, gia ure da giam manh ve muc binh thuong 2023-2024. DPM dang o giai doan day-phuc hoi chu ky. Xem xet mua DPM khi: (1) Gia ure da giam du ve muc co the tang tro lai, (2) Cung ure toan cau giam (Trung Quoc han che xuat khau), (3) Mua vu nong nghiep dang den gan va ton kho thap. Khong mua o dinh chu ky (2022) khi EPS co ve rat cao.

Nhan to dac biet: Chinh sach nhap khau phan bon

Rui ro phan bon nhap khau gia re

Viet Nam nhap khau mot phan phan bon tu Trung Quoc, Indo, Trung Dong. Khi gia ure nhap khau giam manh (do Trung Quoc pha gia), DPM bi canh tranh ngay tren san nha. Bo Cong Thuong co the ap dung thue chong ban pha gia de bao ve, nhung qua trinh nay mat thoi gian va khong chac chan. Day la rui ro dai han can theo doi.

7. Dinh gia

Phan tich Normalized EPS - Bat buoc cho co phieu chu ky

EPS theo nam (dong):
  2019: 1,320
  2020: 1,020
  2021: 2,550
  2022: 5,450 (ky luc - chua tinh thue)
  2023: 1,620

EPS trung binh chu ky = (1,320+1,020+2,550+5,450+1,620)/5 = 2,392

Normalized P/E target cho DPM: 8-12x (chu ky nong san/phan bon)
→ Gia tri noi tai (trung binh): 2,392 x 10 = 23,920 dong

→ Neu gia thi truong < 20,000 dong: BUY vung
→ Neu gia thi truong = 25,000 - 35,000 dong: HOLD
→ Neu gia thi truong > 45,000 dong: Can xem xet giam

LUU Y: Dieu chinh target P/E theo vi tri chu ky:
  - Dang o day chu ky (gia ure thap): Dung P/E thap hon (8x)
  - Dang phuc hoi: Dung P/E trung binh (10x)
  - Dang o dinh: Chi tran P/E cao nhat (12x) voi gia EPS normalize

8. Ket luan & Khuyen nghi

Diem manh

  • Nha san xuat ure lon nhat VN, co thi phan va thuong hieu ben vung
  • Chi phi san xuat canh tranh nho gia khi dieu tiet tu GAS
  • Mang luoi phan phoi rong (4,500 dai ly) kho bi thay the
  • Dang mo rong sang NPK va cac san pham gia tri gia tang
  • Free cash flow tot, it no, co tuc kha khi gia ure tot

Diem yeu / Rui ro

  • Bien loi nhuan bien dong manh theo chu ky gia ure
  • Rui ro gia khi tang khi GAS dieu chinh gia thi truong
  • Phu thuoc chinh sach xuat khau Trung Quoc (quoc gia pha gia)
  • Chu ky mua vu tao ra bat on doanh thu theo quy
  • Rui ro dai han: Nong nghiep chuyen dich sang cong nghe, giam ure

Khung khuyen nghi

Khi nao mua DPM?

Dieu kien MUA: (1) Gia ure thi truong dang o day chu ky va co dau hieu phuc hoi, (2) Trung Quoc bat dau han che xuat khau ure (hay gia khi EU tang lam EU cat san xuat), (3) Mua vu nong nghiep toan cau sap den (Q4 hang nam la luc nong dan bat dau tich tru), (4) P/B <1.2x hoac Normalized P/E <8x, (5) Ton kho DPM thap.

Tranh DPM khi nao?

TRANH MUA: (1) Vua xong mua ky luc - gia ure dang o $800-900 va co the giam, (2) Trung Quoc cong bo tang xuat khau ure, (3) Gia khi tu nhien chau Au giam manh (EU san xuat tro lai → tang cung), (4) BCTC vua cong bo EPS quy cao ky luc nhung xu huong gia ure da dao chieu, (5) P/E hien tai dang tren 15x (dua tren EPS nam phat dinh).
