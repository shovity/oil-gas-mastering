1. Gioi thieu cong ty

PV Drilling (PVD) la cong ty dich vu khoan dau khi hang dau Viet Nam, thanh lap nam 2001 tu PVN. Cong ty cung cap dich vu khoan cho cac du an tham do - khai thac dau khi tren toan quoc va mo rong ra cac nuoc Dong Nam A, Trung Dong.

Lich su phat trien

2001: Thanh lap tu PVN, bat dau voi 1 gian khoan thue
2006: So huu gian khoan dau tien (PVD I)
2009: Niem yet HOSE, von hoa dat ~15,000 ty
2011-2014: Giai doan vang - gia dau >$100/thung, fleet 6 rig
2015-2016: Suy thoai lan 1 - gia dau sup do, PVD lo nang
2017-2019: Phuc hoi cham, sac xep lai danh muc rig
2020-2021: Covid lam gia dau giam, PVD kho khan tiem luc
2022-2023: Phuc hoi manh - gia dau cao, backlog tang
2024+:     Ky vong cu ky len khi PVN tang CAPEX tham do

Vi the trong nganh

  • Cong ty khoan dau khi duy nhat niem yet tai Viet Nam
  • Nam giu ~60-70% thi phan dich vu khoan trong nuoc
  • Doi rig hien tai: 5-6 jack-up rig (1 deep-water semi-sub)
  • Canh tranh truc tiep: Vantage, Noble, Valaris (nuoc ngoai) khi vao VN
Dac diem co cau so huu

PVN so huu ~51% PVD, dam bao loi the ve hop dong uu tien tu cac du an cua tap doan me (PVEP, Vietsovpetro). Day la "moat" quan trong nhat cua PVD - duoc chi dinh thau khoan tren cac mo PVN dang khai thac. Tuy nhien, no cung tao ra rui ro chu sach: quyet dinh dau tu cua PVN anh huong truc tiep den don hang cua PVD.

2. Mo hinh kinh doanh

San pham / Dich vu chinh

Dich vuMo ta% Doanh thu
Cho thue gian khoan (Rig leasing) Jack-up rig tren bien, semi-submersible nuoc sau ~70-75%
Dich vu khoan tron goi (Turnkey) PVD chiu trach nhiem toan bo du an khoan ~15-20%
Dich vu ky thuat khoan Cung cap nhan su, dich vu ky thuat, phan mem khoan ~5-10%

Khach hang

  • PVEP (PVN Exploration & Production) - khach hang lon nhat, ~40-50% doanh thu
  • Vietsovpetro - lien doanh PVN-Nga, khoan mo Bach Ho
  • Zarubezhneft, CNOOC, Petronas - khach hang nuoc ngoai tai VN va bien ngoai
  • Aramco (Saudi Arabia) - PVD da trung thau 1-2 du an o Trung Dong

Dac diem doanh thu

Cach doc doanh thu PVD

Doanh thu PVD = So rig hoat dong x Day rate (USD/ngay) x So ngay hoat dong. Ba bien so nay bien dong manh theo chu ky dau khi. Khi gia dau cao, cac cong ty dau khi tang CAPEX tham do → nhu cau rig tang → day rate tang + utilization tang → doanh thu PVD tang theo ham so nhan.

Co cau tai san

  • Tai san co dinh (rig fleet): ~60-70% tong tai san - khau hao nhanh, can theo doi lich su bao tri

Chinh sach co tuc

PVD tra co tuc khong deu. Giai doan 2015-2021 khong tra co tuc (lo/tinh tru lo). Giai doan 2022-2023 bat dau tra lai 5-10% menh gia. PVD khong phai co phieu co tuc - dau tu vi vo chu ky, khong phai thu nhap thu dong.

4. Metrics dac thu: Rig Count, Day Rate, Utilization

4 so can theo doi moi quy

De phan tich PVD, ban can nam ro 4 chi so sau - chung quyet dinh doanh thu hon bat ky chi so tai chinh nao khac.

4.1 Rig Count (So luong gian khoan)

Fleet PVD (vi du, co the thay doi):
  PVD I    - Jack-up rig, nuoc sau 91m, khoan to 7,000m
  PVD II   - Jack-up rig, nuoc sau 91m
  PVD III  - Jack-up rig, nuoc sau 110m
  PVD V    - Jack-up rig, nuoc sau 120m (thue dai han)
  TAD      - Semi-submersible, nuoc sau 1,500m (nuoc sau nhat)
  + 1-2 rig thue ngan han khi demand cao

-> Fleet lon + modern = cong suat lon va day rate cao hon

4.2 Day Rate (Gia thue theo ngay)

Loai rigDay rate thap (khi xau)Day rate cao (khi tot)Vi du 2023
Jack-up rig (VN)$50,000/ngay$120,000/ngay~$80,000-100,000
Jack-up rig (quoc te)$60,000/ngay$150,000/ngay~$100,000-130,000
Semi-sub nuoc sau$150,000/ngay$400,000/ngay~$200,000-250,000

4.3 Utilization Rate (Ty le su dung)

Utilization = So ngay co hop dong / Tong so ngay co the hoat dong

- >90%: Rat tot - rig hau nhu khong nghi
- 75-90%: Tot - mot so downtime bao tri, di chuyen
- 50-75%: Trung binh - co nguon thu nhung ton chi phi cao
- <50%: Xau - co dinh qua cao, rig "nam khong"
- 0%: Warm/Cold stacked - rig tam thoi ngung, tiet kiem chi phi

PVD 2020-2021: Utilization ~55-65% (rat kho)
PVD 2023: Utilization ~90-95% (phuc hoi manh)

4.4 Backlog (Don hang ton dong)

Backlog = Tong gia tri hop dong da ky chua thuc hien. Day la chi so du bao suc khoe tuong lai cua PVD.

  • Backlog cao (>12 thang doanh thu): An toan, doanh thu co the thay truoc
  • Backlog thap (<6 thang): Rui ro, PVD can ky hop dong moi nhanh
  • Backlog PVD cuoi 2023 uoc ~$500-600M - khoang 12-15 thang doanh thu

5. Phan tich chu ky

Vi tri trong chu ky dau khi (2024)

Nhan dinh chu ky

PVD dang o giai doan phuc hoi - tang truong cua chu ky. Sau day nam suy thoai (2015-2021), gia dau phuc hoi tren $70-80/thung tu 2022 da kich thich cac cong ty dau khi tang CAPEX tham do. Trong khi do, rig fleet toan cau da giam do it dau tu moi tu 2015-2022, tao ra thieu hut cung cap rig. Ket qua: day rate tang nhanh, utilization cao.

Tuong quan voi gia dau

Gia dau Brent → Quyet dinh CAPEX E&P (sau 6-12 thang) → Nhu cau rig (sau 6-12 thang) → Doanh thu PVD

Tri Lag (do tre): ~12-24 thang
→ Khi gia dau bat dau tang, phai doi 1-2 nam PVD moi thay ro loi ich
→ Khi gia dau giam, PVD van con hop dong du lich (backlog) them 1-2 nam

Gia dau de theo doi:
- Tren $80/thung: E&P companies dau tu manh, tich cuc cho PVD
- $60-80/thung: Trung binh, dau tu on dinh
- Duoi $60/thung: Cac du an bi cat giam, rui ro cho PVD

Xu huong ngan/trung han

  • Ngan han (6-12 thang): Backlog hien tai dam bao doanh thu; theo doi them hop dong moi
  • Trung han (1-3 nam): Phu thuoc quyet dinh CAPEX PVN 2024-2026 va gia dau duy tri >$70
  • Dai han: San luong khai thac dau VN giam dan - PVD can mo rong ra nuoc ngoai

Theo doi CAPEX PVN

Khi nao mua PVD?

Signal tot nhat de mua PVD: PVN cong bo tang CAPEX tham do cho nam tiep theo, kem theo gia dau duy tri >$70/thung. Day la luc backlog PVD se bat dau tang. Nen mua khi tin tuc chua chinh thuc hoa vao gia co phieu (thuong 3-6 thang truoc khi thay ro trong BCTC).

7. Dinh gia

Phuong phap dinh gia phu hop

Dung P/E thuong cho co phieu chu ky

P/E cua PVD bien dong rat manh theo chu ky - co the 5x khi dinh va am khi day. Thay vao do, nen dung EV/EBITDA hoac P/B de so sanh. Hoac dung Normalized EPS (EPS trung binh chu ky) thay vi EPS hien tai.

Phuong phapHe so (khi xau)He so (khi tot)So sanh quoc te
EV/EBITDA3-5x8-12xNganh khoan quoc te: 6-10x
P/B0.5-0.8x1.5-2.5xTrung binh: 1x khi binh thuong
P/EN/A (lo)8-15xBien dong qua lon de dung
Normalized P/E10-15x EPS trung binh chu kyPhuong phap tot nhat

Vi du tinh Normalized Valuation

Buoc 1: Tinh EPS trung binh chu ky (5 nam)
  EPS 2019: 400 dong
  EPS 2020: -700 dong
  EPS 2021: -400 dong
  EPS 2022: 1,050 dong
  EPS 2023: 1,500 dong
  EPS trung binh = (400-700-400+1,050+1,500)/5 = 370 dong

Buoc 2: Ap dung Normalized P/E
  P/E target = 12x (trung binh nganh)
  Gia tri noi tai = 370 x 12 = 4,440 dong/co phieu

→ Neu gia hien tai thap hon gia tri noi tai = hap dan
→ Cau hoi: Chu ky hien tai dang o dau? → dieu chinh P/E target

8. Ket luan & Khuyen nghi

Diem manh

  • Vi the doc ton trong nuoc - duoc PVN uu tien hop dong
  • Fleet rig da dang, kha nang phuc vu ca nuoc nong va nuoc sau
  • Kinh nghiem van hanh qua nhieu chu ky, doi ngu ky su giau kinh nghiem
  • Dang huong loi tu xu huong thieu rig toan cau 2022-2026

Diem yeu / Rui ro

  • Phu thuoc qua nhieu vao PVN va gia dau → chu ky bien dong manh
  • Rig fleet dang gia hoa, can dau tu thay moi
  • Canh tranh tu cac cong ty khoan nuoc ngoai khi demand cao
  • Rui ro ty gia USD/VND (doanh thu USD, chi phi partly VND)
  • San luong khai thac dau VN giam dai han → thi truong trong nuoc thu hep

Khung khuyen nghi

Mua PVD khi nao?

Dieu kien MUA: (1) Gia dau Brent duy tri >$70/thung, (2) PVN cong bo tang CAPEX tham do, (3) Backlog PVD >12 thang doanh thu, (4) Gia co phieu dang o day chu ky (P/B <1x hoac P/E thap hon Normalized), (5) Utilization fleet >80% va day rate tang.

Ban / Tranh PVD khi nao?

Dieu kien BAN/TRANH: (1) Gia dau giam duoi $60/thung va xu huong giam tiep, (2) PVN cat giam ke hoach tham do, (3) Backlog giam manh, (4) Gia co phieu da phuc hoi va P/E >15x EPS normalize, (5) Cac hop dong lon se het han trong 6 thang ma chua ky gia han.
